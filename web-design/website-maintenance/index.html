What is Website Maintenance?

Website maintenance the practice of consistently ensuring a website's functionality, security, and relevance stay up to date. This includes updating content, software, SEO, and security measures to keep the site optimized and secure. Conducting ongoing maintenance enhances user experience, helps your business’s online reliability, and much more.
