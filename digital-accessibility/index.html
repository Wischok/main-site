What is Digital Accessibility

Digital accessibility, in a nutshell, is making digital content and sergvices that can be accessed and used by everyone. These come from international and legal standards; it’s important to have a digital accessiblity specialist who can look over your web content for accessiblity.

How do we check for Digital Accessibility?

We check for Digital Accessibility using automated tools and manual testing. Here are a few from our digital accessibility specialists :

  • Online tools such as WAVE. ANDi, and Lighthouse are used to get quick insights.
  • Screen readers are used from beginning to end during ADA website remediation.
  • Text must also be accessible. We check wording for good words
  • Keyboard only: We use only a keyboard to navigate to ensure it’s good too.
